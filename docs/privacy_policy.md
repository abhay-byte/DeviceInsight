# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **DeviceInsight** ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Android application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**DeviceInsight is designed with privacy as a priority. All system monitoring, hardware analysis, and process management operations are performed entirely locally on your device.** 

We **do not** collect, transmit, store, share, or sell your personal data, system usage statistics, or hardware information to any external servers or third parties. The app functions entirely offline.

## 2. Permissions Required

To provide its comprehensive system monitoring functionality, DeviceInsight requires specific Android permissions. We use these permissions strictly to display metrics and manage processes locally:

*   **Package Usage Stats (`PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`)**: Required to monitor running applications and their CPU/RAM usage. This data is only used to populate the Task Manager and is never transmitted off your device.
*   **System Alert Window (`SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW`)**: Required to draw the Performance Overlay (HUD) over other applications or games. 
*   **Battery Statistics (`BATTERY_STATS`)**: Required to read real-time power consumption, wattage, and battery health data.
*   **Phone State (`READ_PHONE_STATE`)**: Required to gather basic hardware identification information (such as SoC model and capabilities).
*   **Root Access (Optional)**: If explicitly granted by the user, root access is used exclusively to provide advanced process control (e.g., force-killing processes) and deeper hardware metric reading (like low-level GPU frequencies). 

## 3. Third-Party Services

DeviceInsight does not integrate with any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The application does not display advertisements.

## 4. Security

Because all data processing happens locally on your device and no data is transmitted over the internet, your system information remains inherently secure and entirely under your control.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
